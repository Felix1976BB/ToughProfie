My Vision of QA/V&V in the Future

The QA field is facing the sharpest changes
it never has seen in the past.
Artificial intelligence and neural networks
for QA engineers offer them brilliant
tools enhancing their capabilities, making them think creatively, allowing them to
switch to exploratory testing, and strategic
decision-making rather than repetitive,
low-scale manual tasks.
And this is not a distant future.
This shift is happening here and now...

Will AI Replace Software Quality Assurance
(SQA) Roles? Some QA specialists claim it
will do! They are afraid of losing their
jobs to AI. However, in fact the mass
introducing of AI to the field of QA, V&V
and testing will actually create new types
of jobs. The requirements to QA specialists
will change. But the demand for this new kind
of specialists, open-minded and innovative ones
who are ready for a sharp change, will grow
explosively in the coming years.

Rather than being afraid of AI, those
QA professionals should change their
negative approach and take an
opportunity to enter a totally new
field of knowledge, to enhance their
skills and become experts in AI.
Instead of fearing the progress,
they should become it's driver!
